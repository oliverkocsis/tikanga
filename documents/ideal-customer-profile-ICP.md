# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Purpose

Define the **highest-value customer segment** most likely to:

* Convert efficiently
* Retain
* Expand
* Advocate
* Deliver strong LTV/CAC

This is a strategic filtering tool, not a demographic description.

## How to Build It (Instructions)

1. Pull top 20% of customers by LTV.
2. Identify patterns across:

   * Revenue
   * Retention
   * Expansion
3. Conduct structured interviews.
4. Validate with sales team.
5. Eliminate segments that are “easy to acquire but low lifetime value.”

## Common Mistakes

* Confusing “any buyer” with “best buyer.”
* Defining ICP based on traffic instead of revenue.
* Ignoring retention data.
* Making it too broad.

## How to Build It

1. Align with product roadmap.
2. Validate demand before full launch.
3. Run pre-launch acquisition tests.
4. Enable sales before launch day.
5. Monitor first 90 days intensely.

## Common Mistakes

* Launching without sales alignment.
* Over-indexing on awareness.
* No post-launch iteration plan.


# Market Research

## Purpose

* Market size
* Growth rate
* Competitive intensity
* Buyer demand
* Structural trends

This informs strategic prioritization.

## How to Build It

1. Combine:

   * Analyst reports
   * SEO data
   * Job board data
   * Industry publications
2. Interview:

   * Customers
   * Non-customers
   * Lost deals
3. Map competitors across:

   * Price
   * Depth
   * Enterprise readiness
4. Validate TAM assumptions conservatively.

## Common Mistakes

* Overestimating TAM.
* Ignoring substitute solutions.
* Using outdated analyst data.
* Copying competitor messaging.
